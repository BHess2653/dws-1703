About Me

I can model, UV, texture, rig, animate and build environments. I work well in a team and can get work done in a timely fashion. When working on Kartastrphe, I worked with a small group of artists who each designed different components of the game. Despite working independently, we still needed to match styles and collaborate. Working on The Legendary Blacksmith was a bit different. The team already had concept art so I needed to create assets to their designs rather than my own. Working on both these games have given me different experiences I believe can help me in the future.
